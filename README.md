<H1> FocusRead — Privacy Policy </H1>  
Last updated: March 2026  
FocusRead is a Chrome browser extension that provides a distraction-free reading mode. This privacy policy explains what data FocusRead does and does not collect.  
We collect nothing.  
FocusRead does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user information of any kind. No data ever leaves your browser.  
<H3> How FocusRead works </H3>
FocusRead reads the text content of web pages you choose to activate it on, solely for the purpose of displaying that content in a clean reading view within your browser. This processing happens entirely on your device. No page content, no reading history, and no usage data is sent to any server.
<H3> What is stored locally </H3>
FocusRead saves your preferences — such as your chosen theme, font size, and font family — to your browser's local storage using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage API. This data never leaves your device and is only used to remember your settings between sessions.
If you activate a PRO licence, your licence key is stored locally in your browser to keep PRO features unlocked. This key is verified against Gumroad's licence API at the time of activation only.
<H3> Third party services </H3>
FocusRead uses Gumroad's licence verification API solely to validate PRO licence keys at the time of purchase activation. No browsing data is shared with Gumroad.
<H3> Children's privacy </H3>
FocusRead does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
<H3> Changes to this policy </H3>
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
